Research Interests

My research interests include Embedded Systems, Emerging Non-volatile Memory, Storage Systems, Real-Time and Embedded Software, Cloud Computing, High performance computing.

Short bio

I am currently an Assistant Professor in the High Performance Computing Research Institute of Shenzhen University. I obtained my Bachelor's degree and PhD degree in 2014 and 2019 both from Hong Kong Polytechnic University (HKPU), under the supervision of Dr. Zili Shao.
